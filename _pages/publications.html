* Yujia Luo, Fang Zheng, Yuanyuan Gao, Wenyu Chen, Xinrui Xue, Chujie Xiao, Kun Wei. Flower-Like MnO2 Nanoparticle-Based Dual GSH-Depleting Nanosystems for Chemo-/Chemodynamic/Sonodynamic Cancer Therapy. ACS Applied Nano Materials, Volume 7, 2024.

This study introduces a novel flower-like MnO2 nanoparticle-based nanosystem that leverages dual GSH-depletion to enhance chemo-, chemodynamic, and sonodynamic therapies for cancer. The proposed system demonstrates superior tumor-targeting capabilities and significant therapeutic efficacy, highlighting its potential for future clinical applications.
